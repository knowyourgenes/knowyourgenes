What is KYG

Okay, but what exactly is a DNA test?

Think of your DNA as the original instruction manual that came with your body at birth. It contains everything about you. How your heart works. How your body fights disease. How your liver breaks down medicines. How likely you are to develop certain conditions in the future. A DNA test reads parts of that instruction manual and tells you what it says. In plain language. Not in doctor language. In your language.

KYG does not tell you what is wrong with you. It tells you what to look after, what to improve, and what to protect for the rest of your life.
