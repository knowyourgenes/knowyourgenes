  00  Journey Overview - How It Works in 6 Steps


The KYG D2C journey is built around maximum convenience and minimum friction. There is no physical kit to mail. The user books a test, a trained professional arrives at their location, the sample is collected, the lab processes the report, and the user receives a clear, jargon-free report. They can then book a consultation with a genetic counselor to understand and act on their results.

Stage 4 - At-Home Sample Collection (The Physical Stage)


NOTE  This is the only physical touchpoint in the entire journey. The phlebotomist or trained professional IS the brand at this moment. Their professionalism, punctuality, and bedside manner directly determine the customer&apos;s trust and likelihood of repeat purchase and referral.





Sub-Step


What Happens


Standard Required


Arrival


Professional arrives within the booked time window. Identifies themselves with name + KYG ID card.


On time within window. Uniformed. ID badge visible. Polite greeting.


Identity Verification


Professional asks user to confirm name and Booking ID. Scans QR code on user&apos;s confirmation WhatsApp.


Mandatory - no Booking ID = no sample collected.


Explain the Process


Brief 2-minute explanation of what will happen. What sample is being collected (blood draw / saliva / swab). How long it takes.


Must be explained in user&apos;s preferred language - Hindi or English minimum.


Consent Form


User signs a digital consent form on the professional&apos;s device. User&apos;s copy emailed/WhatsApp&apos;d instantly.


Mandatory. No sample collected without signed consent.


Sample Collection


Sample collected using sterile, single-use equipment. Proper labelling with Booking ID + user name + date + time.


Zero cross-contamination protocol. NABL-compliant collection procedure.


Post-Collection Care


Blood draw: cotton + bandage applied. User is asked to sit for 2 minutes. Water offered if available.


Professional carries basic first aid. No rushing the user post-draw.


Sample Labelling + Sealing


Sample sealed in biohazard-compliant bag. Labelled with Booking ID. Chain of custody document initiated.


Tamper-evident seal. Booking ID matches the label - checked twice.


Departure &amp; Notification


Professional thanks user. Immediately triggers WhatsApp: &apos;Your sample has been collected and is on its way to our lab. Booking ID: [ID].&apos;


Notification sent within 5 minutes of collection completion.





Phlebotomist Quality Standards (Operations to Define)


Standard


Requirement


Qualification


Certified phlebotomist or DMLT (Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology). Minimum 1 year experience.


Training


KYG-specific training: brand communication, consent process, digital tools, customer handling, emergency protocols.


Equipment


Sterile single-use needles / collection tubes / swabs. Biohazard collection bag. Digital consent tablet/phone. KYG ID card. Uniform / branded attire.


Punctuality SLA


Must arrive within the booked time window. If delayed by &gt; 30 minutes, customer notified and offered reschedule or ₹200 discount.


Behavior Standard


Polite, professional, non-judgmental. Must not discuss test results or give any health opinion. Must speak in user&apos;s language preference.


Background Check


Police verification mandatory. KYG retains records.

Stage 6 - Post-Report Consultation (Optional, Paid)


NOTE  This is NOT part of the test price. It is a separate, premium paid service. However, it is the highest-value interaction in the entire journey - it drives upsells, referrals, and LTV. The &apos;Book a Consultation&apos; CTA must appear on the report page, the delivery WhatsApp message, and in the follow-up sequence.





Element


Detail


What is Offered


30-minute or 60-minute video consultation with a KYG-certified genetic counselor or specialist doctor. Available in English, Hindi, and regional languages.


Who Conducts It


Certified Genetic Counselors (M.Sc. Genetic Counseling, BGCI certified). For clinical results: specialist doctors (oncologist / cardiologist / gynaecologist) as needed.


Pricing


30-minute session: ₹999 – ₹1,499. 60-minute session: ₹1,999 – ₹2,499. (Exact pricing to be set by client. Agency to build pricing page and booking flow.)


Booking Flow


User clicks &apos;Book Consultation&apos; on report page → Calendar with available counselor slots → Select language preference → Pay → Zoom/Google Meet link sent via WhatsApp + Email.


What Happens in the Session


Counselor walks through report findings in plain language. Answers all user questions. Recommends follow-up tests if needed. Provides personalized action plan document post-call.


Post-Consultation


Counselor sends a written Action Plan within 24 hours: 3–5 specific health actions the user should take based on their results.


Upsell Opportunity (Counselor Script)


During the call, counselor identifies additional tests the user or their family may benefit from. Refers them with a family discount code.


Key Metric


Report-to-consultation booking rate &gt; 30%. Consultation CSAT &gt; 4.5/5. Post-consultation upsell conversion &gt; 15%.
