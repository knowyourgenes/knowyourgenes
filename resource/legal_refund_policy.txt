REFUND & CANCELLATION POLICY - KnowYourGenes
Route: /refunds
Last updated: 2026-05-12
================================================================

This Refund & Cancellation Policy applies to all bookings made on
kyg.in or the KnowYourGenes app. It is published in compliance
with Rule 5(3) of the Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules,
2020. By making a payment on the Platform, you agree to this
policy.

We have written this policy plainly. The goal is that you should
not have to read it twice to know where you stand.


1. CANCELLATION BY YOU (BEFORE SAMPLE COLLECTION)
-------------------------------------------------
You may cancel a booking yourself from your order page at any time
before the phlebotomist is dispatched.

  - Cancelled at least 4 hours before the scheduled slot:
        Full refund (100%) to the original payment method.

  - Cancelled between 1 and 4 hours before the slot:
        Refund of the booking amount less a flat ₹200 dispatch
        coordination fee, refunded to the original payment method.

  - Cancelled less than 1 hour before the slot, OR no-show
    (you are unavailable at the address):
        Refund of 50% of the booking amount. The remaining 50%
        is retained as a phlebotomist visit cost.

  - Cancellation after the sample has been collected and sent
    to the lab:
        Not eligible for refund. Lab processing has begun.


2. CANCELLATION BY US
---------------------
We will issue a full refund (100%) without any deduction if:
  - We are unable to dispatch a phlebotomist to your pincode.
  - The slot you booked becomes unavailable and you do not wish
    to reschedule.
  - Our partner lab is unable to process the test type you
    selected.
  - We discover at intake that you are below 18 with no
    parent/guardian present, or the photo ID does not match —
    and you choose not to proceed under a corrected booking.

If we cancel at any point after sample collection (for example,
lab error, sample contamination, sequencing failure), we will at
your option either re-collect at no charge or refund 100% of the
amount paid.


3. SAMPLE INTEGRITY ISSUES
--------------------------
Approximately 1 in 200 samples cannot be processed due to factors
outside our control (insufficient DNA yield, contamination during
transport, etc.). In these cases:

  - We will contact you within 5 business days of the sample
    reaching the lab.
  - We will offer a free re-collection at a slot of your choice.
  - If you prefer not to re-collect, we will refund 100%.


4. REFUND OF REPORTS
--------------------
A genetic report cannot be "returned". We do not refund a
delivered report based solely on the customer's interpretation of
the findings, dissatisfaction with the result, or a change of
mind. Refunds against a delivered report are limited to:

  - Deficiency in the report (incorrect biomarker reported,
    missing section, data corruption): full or partial refund at
    our discretion after review, plus re-issue of the corrected
    report at no charge.

  - Late delivery beyond the stated turnaround time without our
    proactive communication: at your option, a 25% partial refund
    and the report when ready, or 100% refund and we abandon the
    order.

  - Demonstrable laboratory error: 100% refund and free
    re-collection.

Report-related refund claims must be raised within 7 calendar days
of report delivery, by writing to care@kyg.in with a description
of the issue.


5. REFUND TIMELINE & METHOD
---------------------------
  - Refund processing is initiated within 3 business days of
    eligibility being confirmed.
  - Refunds are issued to the original payment method used at
    checkout. UPI and net banking refunds typically reflect in
    5–7 business days; card refunds may take up to 10 business
    days depending on the issuing bank.
  - If the original method is not available (for example, the
    card has expired), we will request alternative bank details
    and refund via NEFT/UPI.
  - We do not refund through cheque or cash.


6. COUPONS & DISCOUNTS
----------------------
  - Refunded amounts are net of any coupon, promotional discount,
    or store credit that was applied to the original payment.
  - A used coupon is restored to your account only if the booking
    is fully cancelled by us before sample collection.
  - Store credit issued by KYG (for example, as goodwill or
    referral reward) is non-refundable to a bank account.


7. PRE-TEST / POST-TEST COUNSELLING
-----------------------------------
Counselling sessions are non-refundable once held. If you cancel a
scheduled counselling session at least 12 hours in advance, the
session is rescheduled at no charge. A no-show or cancellation
inside 12 hours forfeits the session fee.


8. HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND
--------------------------
You can request a refund or cancellation in two ways:

  - From your order page on kyg.in or the app, using the
    "Cancel order" or "Report an issue" button.
  - By writing to care@kyg.in with your order ID, registered
    mobile number, and a brief description of the reason.

We will acknowledge your request within 1 business day and confirm
the outcome within 5 business days.


9. ESCALATION
-------------
If you are not satisfied with how a refund request has been
handled, write to grievance@kyg.in. Our Grievance Officer will
review and respond within 15 days. You retain all your statutory
rights under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019.
