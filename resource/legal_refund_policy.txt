REFUND & CANCELLATION POLICY — KnowYourGenes
Route: /refunds
Last updated: 2026-05-14
================================================================

This Refund & Cancellation Policy applies to all orders placed
on kyg.in or the KnowYourGenes app. It is published in compliance
with Rule 5(3) of the Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules,
2020. By making a payment on the Platform, you agree to this
policy.

We have written this plainly. Cancellation windows are pegged to
the kit lifecycle — what stage your order is in determines what
refund you are eligible for.


1. CANCELLATION BY YOU
----------------------
You may cancel your order from the "My orders" page at any time
before the kit reaches you. The refund amount depends on the
order's status at the time of cancellation.

  - BEFORE the kit is dispatched (status: BOOKED)
      Full refund (100%) to the original payment method.

  - AFTER the kit has been dispatched but BEFORE it is delivered
    (status: KIT_DISPATCHED)
      Refund of the booking amount LESS the actual forward
      courier cost (typically ₹150 to ₹350 depending on your
      pincode). We do not charge any additional fee.

  - AFTER the kit is delivered but BEFORE you register it /
    schedule the reverse pickup (status: KIT_DELIVERED)
      Refund of 50% of the booking amount. The remaining 50%
      covers the forward courier, kit consumables, and
      reverse-courier slot allocation.

  - AFTER the reverse pickup has been scheduled or completed
    (status: SAMPLE_PICKED_UP / SAMPLE_IN_TRANSIT)
      Not eligible for refund. Lab handling is now irreversibly
      in motion.

  - AFTER the sample reaches the lab (status: AT_LAB or beyond)
      Not eligible for refund. Lab processing has begun.


2. CANCELLATION BY US
---------------------
We will issue a full refund (100%) without any deduction if:

  - We are unable to ship a kit to your pincode for any reason.
  - Our partner lab is unable to process the test type you
    selected.
  - We discover at intake that the patient is below 18 with no
    parent/guardian consent on file, and you choose not to
    proceed under a corrected booking.
  - The kit is returned to origin after the courier's maximum
    attempts (typically 3) and you do not respond to our attempts
    to confirm a corrected address within 7 days.

If we cancel at any point after the sample has been picked up
(for example, lab error, sample contamination, sequencing
failure), we will at your option either dispatch a free
re-collection kit or refund 100% of the amount paid.


3. SAMPLE INTEGRITY ISSUES
--------------------------
Approximately 1 in 200 samples cannot be processed due to factors
outside our control (insufficient DNA yield, contamination during
transport, degraded sample, etc.). In these cases:

  - We will contact you within 5 business days of the sample
    reaching the lab.
  - We will dispatch a free re-collection kit at our cost.
  - If you prefer not to re-collect, we will refund 100%.


4. LOST OR UNDELIVERABLE KITS
-----------------------------
  - If the FORWARD kit is lost in courier transit, we re-ship
    at our cost as soon as the courier confirms loss. Your order
    TAT clock resets to the date of the second dispatch.

  - If the REVERSE-leg shipment is lost in transit, we treat it
    as a sample integrity issue (clause 3) and dispatch a free
    re-collection kit.

  - If the kit is returned to origin (RTO) after maximum delivery
    attempts, we contact you to confirm a corrected address. We
    re-ship at our cost the first time. If a second RTO occurs,
    you may choose between a third dispatch with an actuals
    shipping fee, or cancellation under clause 1.


5. REFUND OF REPORTS
--------------------
A genetic report cannot be "returned". We do not refund a
delivered report based solely on the customer's interpretation
of the findings, dissatisfaction with the result, or a change of
mind. Refunds against a delivered report are limited to:

  - Deficiency in the report (incorrect biomarker reported,
    missing section, data corruption): full or partial refund
    at our discretion after review, plus re-issue of the
    corrected report at no charge.

  - Late delivery beyond the stated end-to-end TAT without our
    proactive communication: at your option, a 25% partial
    refund and the report when ready, or 100% refund and we
    abandon the order.

  - Demonstrable laboratory error: 100% refund and free
    re-collection.

Report-related refund claims must be raised within 7 calendar
days of report delivery, by writing to care@kyg.in with a
description of the issue.


6. REFUND TIMELINE & METHOD
---------------------------
  - Refund processing is initiated within 3 business days of
    eligibility being confirmed.
  - Refunds are issued to the original payment method used at
    checkout. UPI and net banking refunds typically reflect in
    5–7 business days; card refunds may take up to 10 business
    days depending on the issuing bank.
  - If the original method is not available (for example, the
    card has expired), we will request alternative bank details
    and refund via NEFT/UPI.
  - We do not refund through cheque or cash.


7. COUPONS & DISCOUNTS
----------------------
  - Refunded amounts are net of any coupon, promotional discount,
    or store credit that was applied to the original payment.
  - A used coupon is restored to your account only if the
    booking is fully cancelled by us before kit dispatch.
  - Store credit issued by KYG (for example, as goodwill or
    referral reward) is non-refundable to a bank account.


8. PRE-TEST / POST-TEST COUNSELLING
-----------------------------------
Counselling sessions are non-refundable once held. If you cancel
a scheduled counselling session at least 12 hours in advance,
the session is rescheduled at no charge. A no-show or
cancellation inside 12 hours forfeits the session fee.


9. HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND
--------------------------
You can request a refund or cancellation in two ways:

  - From your order page on kyg.in or the app, using the
    "Cancel order" or "Report an issue" button.
  - By writing to care@kyg.in with your order ID, registered
    mobile number, and a brief description of the reason.

We will acknowledge your request within 1 business day and
confirm the outcome within 5 business days.


10. ESCALATION
--------------
If you are not satisfied with how a refund request has been
handled, write to grievance@kyg.in. Our Grievance Officer will
review and respond within 15 days. You retain all your statutory
rights under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019.
