RAZORPAY ACCOUNT CREATION & ONBOARDING — GUIDELINES
For: KnowYourGenes (Healthcare > Diagnostic Center category)
================================================================

WHAT RAZORPAY ACTUALLY CHECKS DURING ONBOARDING
-----------------------------------------------
Razorpay's activation team reviews four things in parallel. All four
must clear before live keys are issued.

1. BUSINESS LEGITIMACY
   - Is the legal entity real and registered?
   - Is the entity legally allowed to operate in the chosen category?
   - Does the entity's stated activity match the selected
     category / subcategory on Razorpay?
   - Do PAN, GST, and bank account all resolve to the same entity?

2. WEBSITE / APP COMPLIANCE
   - Is the website live and reachable?
   - Are the mandatory legal pages present, complete, and reachable
     from the footer of every page? (Details below.)
   - Does the site clearly describe what is being sold and at what
     price?

3. SERVICE CLARITY
   - The visitor should be able to answer, within 30 seconds:
       a) What service does this business provide?
       b) How much does it cost / how is it priced?
       c) How do I contact the business?
       d) What is the business's address?

4. CATEGORY-SPECIFIC COMPLIANCE
   - For Healthcare > Diagnostic Center: the Affiliation Certificate
     (see guidelines_affiliate.txt for the full guide).
   - No prohibited medical claims on the site (see below).


DOCUMENTS YOU MUST PROVIDE
--------------------------
Personal KYC (of the authorized signatory):
  - PAN card (individual)
  - Aadhaar — front and back
  - Valid email and Indian mobile number

Business KYC:
  - Business PAN (for Pvt Ltd / LLP / Partnership — the entity PAN,
    not the founder's personal PAN)
  - GST certificate (if registered; mandatory above the GST
    threshold or if voluntarily registered)
  - Certificate of Incorporation / LLP Agreement / Partnership Deed
    (whichever applies to the entity type)
  - MSME / Udyam Registration certificate (optional but helpful)

Bank Proof:
  - Cancelled cheque OR latest bank statement OR bank welcome letter
  - Must show: account holder name (matches business name),
    account number, and IFSC code

Category-specific (Healthcare > Diagnostic Center):
  - Affiliation Certificate — see guidelines_affiliate.txt


MANDATORY WEBSITE PAGES
-----------------------
These pages must exist, be linked from the site footer, and contain
real content (placeholder Lorem Ipsum will trigger rejection). For
healthcare these are scrutinized more strictly than other categories.

  1. Privacy Policy
       - What personal data and health data you collect
       - How you store it, who has access, how long you keep it
       - User rights (access, correction, deletion)
       - Mention of compliance with the DPDP Act, 2023

  2. Terms & Conditions (Terms of Service)
       - Scope of services offered
       - User obligations
       - Disclaimers (results are diagnostic, not a substitute for
         professional medical advice)
       - Limitation of liability
       - Governing law and jurisdiction (an Indian city)

  3. Refund / Cancellation Policy
       - When refunds are issued and when they are not
       - Timeline for refunds (typically 5-7 business days)
       - Cancellation window for sample collection / booked tests
       - Method of refund (back to source)

  4. Shipping / Service Delivery Policy
       - Sample collection geography (which pincodes)
       - Turnaround time for reports
       - How reports are delivered (email, app, portal)

  5. Contact Us
       - Working email (not a personal Gmail if avoidable)
       - Phone number that is actually answered during stated hours
       - Full business address (must match registered address)

  6. About Us
       - Who runs the business, brief background
       - For healthcare: name(s) of the medical / pathology lead and
         their qualification(s) and registration number(s) helps
         enormously with approval


PROHIBITED CONTENT (HEALTHCARE-SPECIFIC)
----------------------------------------
The Drugs and Magic Remedies (Objectionable Advertisements) Act, 1954
and Razorpay's own policy together prohibit certain claims. Any of
these on your site is a near-automatic rejection:

  - "Guaranteed cure" / "100% cure"
  - "Permanent treatment" of any disease in Schedule J of the
    Drugs and Cosmetics Rules (diabetes, cancer, AIDS, hypertension,
    epilepsy, etc.)
  - "Instant results" / "miracle test"
  - Claims to diagnose pregnancy gender (prohibited under PCPNDT Act)
  - Before / after photos implying treatment cure
  - Endorsements by celebrities without medical qualifications
  - Promotion of prescription drugs to consumers
  - Claims that displace consulting a qualified doctor

Keep medical language realistic, evidence-based, and accompanied by
disclaimers ("Consult a qualified healthcare professional before
acting on any test result").


RESTRICTED BUSINESS TYPES (RAZORPAY WILL REJECT)
------------------------------------------------
  - Unlicensed pharmacies / sale of prescription drugs without Rx
  - Misleading "miracle" supplements
  - MLM / pyramid schemes (even if dressed as wellness)
  - Gambling, adult content, illegal services
  - Sex-determination services (criminal offence in India)


COMMON REJECTION REASONS (RANK-ORDERED)
---------------------------------------
  1. Missing or incomplete legal pages (especially refund policy)
  2. Business name mismatch across documents
  3. Website that looks unfinished — broken links, "coming soon"
     sections, placeholder text
  4. Personal savings account submitted instead of current account
     in the business name
  5. Selected category does not match website content
  6. Healthcare claims violating ASCI / Drugs Act
  7. Expired or non-matching affiliation document


ONBOARDING WORKFLOW — RECOMMENDED ORDER
---------------------------------------
  1. Decide legal entity type. For a healthcare platform a Pvt Ltd
     is strongly recommended (limits personal liability and is
     viewed more favorably by Razorpay than a sole proprietorship).
  2. Complete incorporation and obtain PAN, GST, current account.
  3. Build / finish the website. Ship the six mandatory pages with
     real, healthcare-aware content. Test every link.
  4. Sign the partner-lab agreement (if you don't operate your own
     lab). Ensure the partner's NABL/accreditation number appears
     in the agreement.
  5. On Razorpay: select Healthcare > Diagnostic Center, fill KYC,
     upload all documents. Use the cover-note approach from
     guidelines_affiliate.txt if you are early-stage.
  6. Expect 2-5 business days for review. If rejected, the email
     will state the specific reason — fix only that and resubmit.


FAST-APPROVAL BUNDLE (WHAT TO UPLOAD)
-------------------------------------
For a small / early-stage diagnostic platform, the smoothest path is:

  [ ] Business PAN
  [ ] GST certificate
  [ ] Certificate of Incorporation (Pvt Ltd)
  [ ] Cancelled cheque of business current account
  [ ] MSME / Udyam (helpful, not mandatory)
  [ ] Affiliation proof — see guidelines_affiliate.txt
  [ ] Personal PAN + Aadhaar of authorized signatory
  [ ] Live website with all six mandatory pages working
