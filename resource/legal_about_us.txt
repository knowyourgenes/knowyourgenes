HERO

Headline:
   DNA testing, made simple.

Subhead:
   We bring an NABL-accredited genetic test to your doorstep

WHO WE ARE
----------
KnowYourGenes ("KYG") is a brand operated by BFG Market Consult
Private Limited, a company incorporated in India under the
Companies Act, 2013, bearing CIN U74999DL2010PTC207582 and
registered with the Registrar of Companies, NCT of Delhi and
Haryana since 20 August 2010. Our registered office is at
SU 18, Pitam Pura, Delhi 110034.

We are a Delhi NCR–based D2C genetic testing service. We launched
in May 2026 with a single belief: that the friction between you
and an evidence-based genetic test is mostly logistical, and
solvable. Most Indians who could benefit from a DNA test never
take one because the experience is built around clinics, not
patients. We are changing that.


WHAT WE DO
----------
We work with NABL-accredited partner labs and licensed clinical
geneticists to offer panel-based DNA tests covering:

  - Wellness - nutrition, fitness, sleep, stress, vitamin and
    mineral metabolism.
  - Drug sensitivity - pharmacogenomic markers relevant to
    common prescription medicines.
  - Cardiac - inherited risk markers for cardiovascular disease.
  - Cancer risk - hereditary cancer-syndrome screening (BRCA,
    Lynch, and related panels).
  - Reproductive - pre-conception carrier screening and family
    planning panels, offered strictly within the framework of
    the PCPNDT Act, 1994.

A trained phlebotomist visits your address. The sample goes to
the lab. A report comes back to your phone in 10 to 21 business
days depending on the panel. Optional pre-test and post-test
counselling is available with qualified genetic counsellors.


HOW WE'RE DIFFERENT
-------------------
1. At-home, not in-clinic.
   A KYG phlebotomist comes to you, on your schedule.

2. Plain-language reports.
   Reports are written to be read by you first, with a
   structured handover summary if you choose to share with your
   doctor.

3. NABL-accredited processing.
   Every sample is processed at an NABL-accredited partner
   laboratory, under the supervision of a reporting pathologist.

4. Your data, your choice.
   We do not sell your data, do not share with insurers, and do
   not use it to train any third-party AI model. You can have
   your genetic data deleted any time, from your account.

5. Indian data, Indian rules.
   All data is stored and processed in India, in compliance with
   the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.


OUR BELIEFS
-----------
  - Information is not a diagnosis. A report is a starting
    point for a conversation with your doctor - not a verdict.
  - Genetic risk is probabilistic, not predictive. We will not
    tell you that you will get a disease. We will tell you what
    your DNA suggests about predisposition, and what the
    evidence says you can do about it.
  - Counselling matters. Especially for cancer-risk and
    reproductive panels, the report is the easy part - the
    conversation is the important part. We bake in counselling
    where it counts.
