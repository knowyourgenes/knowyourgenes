================================================================
AFFILIATION CERTIFICATE — GUIDELINES
For: Razorpay onboarding, Category = Healthcare, Subcategory = Diagnostic Center
================================================================

WHY RAZORPAY ASKS FOR THIS
--------------------------
When you select Healthcare > Diagnostic Center, Razorpay's compliance
team needs to verify three things before activating payments:

  1. Your diagnostic/lab business is a real, legally registered entity.
  2. You are authorized to collect payments for diagnostic services.
  3. You comply with applicable healthcare regulations in India.

The "Affiliation Certificate" upload slot is where you prove the
business is genuinely connected to a recognized diagnostic / medical
setup. Razorpay does not publish the exact accepted-document list for
this category — the items below are the documents merchants have
successfully used (confirmed via Razorpay support and community
reports). Any ONE of them is usually sufficient.


ACCEPTED DOCUMENTS (UPLOAD ANY ONE YOU HAVE)
--------------------------------------------
Strongest proofs (preferred):
  - NABL accreditation certificate
      (National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration
      Laboratories — gold standard for diagnostic labs)
  - NABH certificate (for hospital-affiliated diagnostic units)
  - Clinical Establishment Registration Certificate
      (issued under the Clinical Establishments Act, 2010, or the
      equivalent State Act — e.g., Bombay Nursing Homes Act,
      Karnataka Private Medical Establishments Act, etc.)
  - Diagnostic Center / Pathology Lab registration certificate
      issued by the State Health Department or municipal authority

Acceptable alternatives:
  - Shop & Establishment License where the listed business activity
    explicitly mentions "diagnostic", "pathology", "lab", or
    "healthcare services"
  - GST certificate where the principal business activity / HSN-SAC
    code maps to healthcare or diagnostic services
  - Partnership deed / Certificate of Incorporation that names
    diagnostic services as a business object
  - Doctor / Pathologist affiliation letter on letterhead
    (signed by an MD Pathology / qualified medical practitioner who
    oversees lab operations, with their registration number)
  - Lab authorization / empanelment certificate

If you are a BOOKING / AGGREGATOR PLATFORM (not running your own lab):
  - Partnership / service agreement with the actual diagnostic lab(s)
    you book through — this is the document you upload as the
    affiliation proof. Multi-lab platforms can upload one agreement
    and list the others in cover note.


WHAT THE DOCUMENT MUST CLEARLY SHOW
-----------------------------------
Razorpay reviewers look for these fields on the page:

  - Business / entity name (legal name, not just brand name)
  - Type of activity — must say diagnostic / lab / pathology /
    healthcare (NOT just "general trading" or "services")
  - Full business address
  - Registration / license / accreditation number
  - Name of issuing authority (NABL, State Health Dept, etc.)
  - Date of issue and validity / expiry date if applicable
  - Signature / seal of the issuing authority

CRITICAL — NAME CONSISTENCY:
The business name on the affiliation document must match (or be a
clear variant of) the name on:
  - Razorpay merchant account
  - PAN card
  - GST certificate
  - Bank account / cancelled cheque

If names differ (e.g., brand name vs. legal entity), upload a one-page
declaration on letterhead linking them, plus board resolution or
partnership minute if applicable.


IF YOU ARE EARLY-STAGE / DON'T HAVE LAB LICENSES YET
----------------------------------------------------
This is the realistic case for many startups. Razorpay does manually
review and approve early-stage healthcare businesses. Upload the
strongest bundle you can assemble:

  1. GST certificate
  2. MSME / Udyam Registration certificate
  3. Certificate of Incorporation (Pvt Ltd) OR LLP agreement OR
     partnership deed
  4. Vendor / partner agreement with at least one accredited
     diagnostic lab (sample collection partner, fulfillment lab, etc.)
  5. Screenshots of the live website showing diagnostic services,
     pricing, and contact details
  6. A cover note (PDF, on letterhead) explaining the model:
     "We are a diagnostic booking platform. Sample collection and
     reporting is performed by partner lab [Name], NABL accredited,
     accreditation # XYZ. Agreement attached."

Bundle these into a single PDF and upload to the Affiliation
Certificate slot. The cover note is the most important piece — it
tells the reviewer what they are looking at.


COMMON MISTAKES THAT TRIGGER REJECTION
--------------------------------------
  - Uploading a generic Shop & Establishment license that doesn't
    mention healthcare / diagnostics
  - Document where the business name doesn't match Razorpay /
    GST / bank records
  - Expired license / accreditation
  - Photo of document at an angle, blurry, or with missing pages
  - Uploading only Aadhaar / PAN (those go in personal KYC, not
    affiliation)
  - Lab partner agreement without both parties' signatures and
    company seals


CHECKLIST BEFORE YOU UPLOAD
---------------------------
  [ ] Document is a clear, full-page scan or PDF (not a phone photo
      with shadows)
  [ ] Business name on document matches Razorpay account
  [ ] Document explicitly mentions diagnostic / lab / healthcare
  [ ] Registration number is visible
  [ ] Document is currently valid (not expired)
  [ ] If using a partner-lab agreement: both parties signed and
      dated, includes lab's NABL/accreditation reference
  [ ] File size under Razorpay's upload limit (typically 5 MB),
      format PDF / JPG / PNG
