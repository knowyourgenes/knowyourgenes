SHIPPING & SERVICE DELIVERY POLICY — KnowYourGenes
Route: /shipping
Last updated: 2026-05-14
================================================================

KnowYourGenes is a doorstep DNA-testing service that operates by
post. When you place an order, we courier a sample-collection kit
to your address. You collect your sample at home using the kit
and the instructions included. We then arrange a reverse pickup,
the sample travels to our partner NABL-accredited lab, and your
report is delivered digitally to your KYG account and email.

This page is also published in compliance with Rule 6 of the
Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules, 2020, which requires us
to disclose service-delivery timelines and modes.


1. SERVICE AREA
---------------
We ship kits across India to pincodes serviced by our courier
partner. The serviceability of your pincode is checked at
checkout. If your pincode is not currently serviceable, the order
will not be permitted and your card will not be charged. You may
join the waitlist for your pincode from the same screen and we
will notify you when we expand.

At-home phlebotomist collection (where a trained professional
visits to draw a blood sample) is on our future roadmap; it is
not currently offered. Today every test uses a self-collected
saliva or buccal swab sample.


2. WHAT HAPPENS AFTER YOU BOOK
------------------------------
Step 1 — Order confirmation
   Within 5 minutes of successful payment you receive an email
   and WhatsApp confirmation containing your order ID, contents
   of your order, and the expected kit-delivery window.

Step 2 — Kit dispatch
   We hand the kit to our courier partner within 1 to 2 business
   days of order confirmation. You receive a tracking link by
   email and WhatsApp once the AWB is generated.

Step 3 — Kit delivery
   The courier delivers the kit to the address you entered at
   checkout, typically within 3 to 7 business days of dispatch
   depending on your pincode. The kit contains a sealed
   collection tube or swab, an identification sticker, written
   instructions, and a pre-paid return label for the reverse
   shipment.

Step 4 — Self-collection at home
   You collect your sample at home by following the instructions
   in the kit. Most users complete this step in under 5 minutes.
   Instructions are also available in your KYG account under
   "My orders".

Step 5 — Kit registration
   Before scheduling the reverse pickup, you register the kit in
   your KYG account by entering the unique kit code printed on
   the identification sticker. This links your sample to your
   account and re-confirms your Genetic Testing Consent.

Step 6 — Reverse pickup
   You schedule the reverse pickup from your dashboard. Our
   courier partner collects the sealed kit from your address,
   typically within 1 to 2 business days of the request. Sample
   tubes are stable at room temperature for the duration of
   transit.

Step 7 — Lab processing
   The sample reaches the partner NABL-accredited lab, typically
   within 3 to 7 business days of pickup. The lab processes the
   sample, generates the variant call file, and produces your
   interpretive report under the supervision of a reporting
   pathologist or clinical geneticist.

Step 8 — Report delivery
   Your report is delivered via a secure link over email and
   WhatsApp, and is also available under "My Reports" in your
   KYG account.


3. TURNAROUND TIME
------------------
End-to-end TAT is the sum of kit dispatch, kit delivery, your own
collection time, reverse pickup, and lab processing. The figure
shown on each package page is the LAB TAT only (from the date
your sample reaches the lab to the date your report is delivered).
Realistic end-to-end ranges by category:

  - Wellness panels       : 14 to 21 business days end-to-end
  - Drug sensitivity      : 14 to 21 business days end-to-end
  - Cardiac panels        : 18 to 28 business days end-to-end
  - Cancer risk panels    : 21 to 32 business days end-to-end
  - Reproductive panels   : 21 to 32 business days end-to-end
  - Comprehensive bundles : up to 35 business days end-to-end

TAT is indicative, not a guarantee. The largest source of
variability is your own collection and pickup-scheduling step —
please complete it promptly to keep your order on schedule.
Clinically necessary lab steps (re-extraction, repeat sequencing,
expert review) may also extend it. We proactively notify you of
any expected delay over WhatsApp and email.


4. TRACKING YOUR ORDER
----------------------
Every transition is reflected in real time in your KYG account
under "My orders": order placed, kit dispatched (with AWB), kit
delivered, kit registered, pickup scheduled, sample in transit,
sample at lab, report ready. You also receive email and WhatsApp
messages at each major milestone.


5. RESCHEDULING AND REROUTING
-----------------------------
You can update the delivery address for your kit at no charge
until the kit has been handed to the courier (Step 2). After
dispatch, address changes must be coordinated with the courier;
redirection may not always be possible. Write to care@kyg.in
with your order ID for help.

You can reschedule the reverse pickup up to the moment the
courier is dispatched, at no charge, from your dashboard.


6. CAN I BUY FOR SOMEONE ELSE?
------------------------------
Yes — you may buy a kit for a parent, child, partner, or friend.
At the point of booking, you add the patient's details. The kit
is shipped to the address you entered. The kit registration step
on the dashboard captures the patient's consent. Reports are sent
to the email and mobile number provided for the patient, unless
you explicitly elect to receive them at the payer's contact for
a minor or dependent adult.


7. WHAT YOU DO NOT RECEIVE
--------------------------
We do NOT post a printed report by default. If you specifically
request a printed, notarised copy of your report for clinical use,
we can courier it to your address for an additional fee on
actuals — write to care@kyg.in.

We do NOT sell or ship physical merchandise, supplements, food,
or drug products through the Platform.


8. FAILED DELIVERY, FAILED SAMPLE, OR LOST SHIPMENT
---------------------------------------------------
If the courier is unable to deliver the kit because the address
is unreachable or no one is available, the kit will be
re-attempted (up to two additional attempts) and then returned
to origin. You may then either provide a corrected address (we
re-ship at our cost the first time) or cancel under our Refund
Policy.

If a reverse-leg shipment is lost in transit, or the sample
arrives at the lab degraded or in insufficient quantity to be
processed (this happens for approximately 1 in 200 samples for
reasons outside our control), we dispatch a free re-collection
kit. If you prefer not to re-collect, we refund 100%.


9. EXTERNAL FACTORS
-------------------
Service may be delayed or paused without prior notice in case of
force majeure events: civil disturbance, natural disaster,
government-mandated lockdowns, courier strikes, lab outages, or
any other event outside our reasonable control. We will
communicate the expected resumption time and offer a no-fee
reschedule or full refund.
