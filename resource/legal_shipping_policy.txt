SHIPPING & SERVICE DELIVERY POLICY — KnowYourGenes
Route: /shipping
Last updated: 2026-05-12
================================================================

KnowYourGenes is a doorstep genetic testing service. We do not
ship a "kit" to your home for you to collect and post back. A
trained KYG phlebotomist visits your address, collects the sample,
transports it to our partner NABL-accredited lab, and we deliver
your report digitally. This page describes how that end-to-end
service works and the timelines you should expect.

This page is also published in compliance with Rule 6 of the
Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules, 2020, which requires us
to disclose service-delivery timelines and modes.


1. SERVICE AREA
---------------
At launch, KnowYourGenes serves the following region:

  - Delhi National Capital Region (Delhi NCR) — Delhi, Gurugram,
    Noida, Greater Noida, Ghaziabad, Faridabad.

The exact list of serviceable pincodes is enforced at checkout. If
your pincode is not serviceable, the order will not be permitted
and your card will not be charged. You may join the waitlist for
your pincode from the same screen and we will notify you when we
expand.


2. WHAT HAPPENS AFTER YOU BOOK
------------------------------
Step 1 — Confirmation
   Within 5 minutes of successful payment you receive a booking
   confirmation over email and WhatsApp, with your order ID,
   selected time slot and the agent assignment status.

Step 2 — Agent assignment
   By the morning of your collection slot (or at least 2 hours
   before, whichever is later), a trained phlebotomist is
   assigned to your booking. You receive their name, photo, and
   contact number over WhatsApp.

Step 3 — Visit
   The phlebotomist arrives at your address within the selected
   slot window. You will be asked to verify identity (photo ID),
   sign a digital consent form, and confirm any pre-collection
   instructions (e.g., fasting status) you were given.

Step 4 — Sample collection
   Collection takes 10 to 20 minutes depending on sample type
   (saliva swab or venous blood). The phlebotomist follows
   standard biosafety protocols.

Step 5 — Lab transit
   The sample is labelled with a de-identified accession number,
   placed in a temperature-controlled transport container, and
   delivered to the partner NABL lab the same day or by the
   following morning.

Step 6 — Lab processing
   The lab processes the sample, generates the variant call file,
   and interprets it under the supervision of a reporting
   pathologist / clinical geneticist.

Step 7 — Report delivery
   Your report is delivered via a secure link over email and
   WhatsApp, and is also available under "My Reports" in your
   KYG account.


3. TURNAROUND TIME
------------------
The turnaround time ("TAT") shown on each package page is the
period from sample receipt at the lab to report delivery. Typical
ranges by category:

  - Wellness panels       : 10 to 14 business days
  - Drug sensitivity      : 10 to 14 business days
  - Cardiac panels        : 12 to 18 business days
  - Cancer risk panels    : 14 to 21 business days
  - Reproductive panels   : 14 to 21 business days
  - Comprehensive bundles : up to 28 business days

TAT is indicative, not a guarantee. Clinically necessary steps
(re-extraction, repeat sequencing, expert review) may extend it.
We proactively notify you of any expected delay over WhatsApp and
email.


4. RESCHEDULING
---------------
You can reschedule a confirmed booking at no charge:
  - Up to 4 hours before the slot — self-service from your
    order page.
  - Inside 4 hours — by writing to care@kyg.in or replying to
    your booking WhatsApp. We will accommodate where possible;
    no fee.

If a phlebotomist arrives and you are unavailable, this counts as
a no-show and the cancellation rules in our Refund Policy apply.


5. CAN I BUY FOR SOMEONE ELSE?
------------------------------
Yes — you may book and pay for a parent, child, partner, or
friend. At the point of booking, you will be asked to add the
patient's details. The phlebotomist will verify the patient's ID,
not the payer's, on arrival. Reports are sent to the email and
mobile number provided for the patient, unless you explicitly
elect to receive them at the payer's contact for a minor or
dependent adult.


6. WHAT YOU DO NOT RECEIVE BY COURIER
-------------------------------------
We do NOT mail you a self-collection kit. We do NOT post a
printed report by default. If you specifically request a printed,
notarised copy of your report for clinical use, we can courier it
to a Delhi NCR address for an additional fee on actuals — write
to care@kyg.in.

We do NOT sell or ship physical merchandise, supplements, or any
food / drug product through the Platform.


7. FAILED COLLECTION
--------------------
If we cannot collect your sample on the scheduled date because:
  - the address is unreachable or you are not present, OR
  - the photo ID does not match the patient on the booking, OR
  - you are unwell in a manner that makes collection unsafe,
the visit is treated as a no-show under our Refund Policy. We
will offer to reschedule within 7 days.


8. EXTERNAL FACTORS
-------------------
Service may be delayed or paused without prior notice in case of
force majeure events: civil disturbance, natural disaster,
government-mandated lockdowns, lab outages, or any other event
outside our reasonable control. We will communicate the expected
resumption time and offer a no-fee reschedule or full refund.
